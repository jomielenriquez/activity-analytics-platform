# Project rules
This is a graded take-home assignment. AI_USAGE.md must contain a real,
unedited log of every session: my prompts, your responses (verbatim or
faithfully complete), and notes on where I corrected or redirected you.

At the end of every work session, append the full session transcript to
AI_USAGE.md before we stop. Do not summarize or clean up prompts — log
them as actually written, including false starts and corrections.
